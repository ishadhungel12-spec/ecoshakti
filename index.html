Our Innovation

Two-part smart system
for a smarter planet.

📷

AI Camera Detector

Trained AI classifies food as reusable or throwable in real time, then sends data and alerts to shelters instantly.

  • Real-time food classification via computer vision
  • Triggers instant Gmail & SMS shelter notifications
  • Tracks donation data on a live web dashboard
  • Supports local shelter and food bank network
🗂️

Waste Segregator

Automated sorting of throwable food into dry and wet compartments for responsible environmental management.

  • Auto-separates dry and wet food waste
  • Integrated with AI camera output signals
  • Prevents harmful waste mixing & pollution
  • Enables proper composting and recycling
